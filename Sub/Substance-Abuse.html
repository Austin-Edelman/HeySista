Our mission
We seek to create awareness of the challenges of incarcerated and formerly incarcerated women. We illuminate the hardship of mental health and substance abuse, the hurdles to education, the unrelenting discrimination, and the outdated legislation we face every day. We seek to turn awareness into action.
