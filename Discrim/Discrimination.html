Disparities

Educate yourselves. Clearly there's an issue with our Court systems.

As you can see in the sentencing graph, blacks are harshly punished more than whites. There's 63% of whites in U.S population and 13% blacks; yet almost 50% of blacks are incarcerated and serving LIFE sentences? It's Disgusting! Reality is, most blacks commited the same crimes as whites; yet the consequences are tremendously different. Felony crimes, which usually warant the life sentences are not the only issue; it's sentencing in a whole. Even with minor charges with lesser sentences, the time WE get vs the time THEY get, are always dissimilar.
