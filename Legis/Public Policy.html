Public Policy

Awareness to Action

Why It Matters
Public policy can create legislation to ensure public safety while preparing women to return to their communities and families for successful reentry. Together, we can break the cycle of incarceration that traumatizes, destroys families, and absorbs taxpayer dollars by helping women become productive citizens.
